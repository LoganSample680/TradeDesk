Help & FAQ
💳 How payments work
Your contractor sends you a secure payment link. You can pay by card, ACH bank transfer, or Apple Pay. Deposits are due before work begins; the final balance is due on completion. All payments are processed securely — your contractor never sees your card details.
✍️ How signing works
When you receive a proposal, tap Review & Sign. You'll see the full scope of work and price. Sign with your finger or mouse and submit. You'll receive a confirmation email with a copy of the signed agreement.
📞 Contacting your contractor
Use the Call, Text, or Email buttons on the Overview tab to reach your contractor directly. For non-urgent questions, the Messages tab keeps everything in one place so nothing gets lost in your inbox.
❓ Common questions
Can I change the scope after signing? Yes — your contractor can send a change order for your approval.

Is this hub link private? Yes. The link is unique to you and not publicly indexed.

How do I get a copy of my invoice? Go to the Payments tab and tap any invoice row to open a printable version.
